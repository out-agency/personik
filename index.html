Reach every employee digitally

Everyone has a smartphone with a variety of messaging apps installed. The only question is how to reach them all at once. And that's where Personik shines!
Personik delivers the information you're willing to share across apps and devices already used by your employees.
So they don't have to install a dozen of corporate apps and you don't have to do the boring stuff manually.

Deliver your information across the company in a single click

Compose your message
Type in some text, add images, polls and step-by-step guides.
Choose recipients

Gather feedback

See it delivered

Nornickel
Nornickel is Russia's leading metals and mining company and the world's largest high-grade nickel and palladium producer.

Employs more than 70 000 people.

Nornickel use Personik to provide instant HR self services and mass notifications via messengers to all employees wherever they work in office at the desk, in mine or at nickel factory. Using Personik via Viber or Telegram every employee can get payslip, vacation limits and dozens other services from smartphone saving hours of their lifetime.

  • More than 40 000 registered users
  • 100 000 requests per month proceed
  • 35 self services for employees and managers
  • 2 hours of production time saved per employee per month
  • More than 20 mass notification campaigns from different branches of business per month
Metalloinvest
Metalloinvest is a leading global producer and supplier of HBI and iron ore products, and a regional producer of high quality steel. Employs more than 65 000 people.

Metalloinvest automate HR self services by Personik to reduce costs for processing common HR requests from employees and provide instant service for basic HR operations. Using Personik via Viber employees of Metalloinvest can get different types of forms, payslips, request vacation or inform about sick leaves.

  • More than 30 000 registered users
  • 85 000 requests per month proceed
  • 15 HR self services for employees available via Viber
  • 2,5 hours of production time saved per employee per month
Gazprom neft
Gazprom neft is the third largest oil producer in Russia and ranked third according to refining throughput. Employs more than 65 000 people.

During transformation to first Russian IT Oil company Gazprom neft acquiring lot of high IT specialists from students to high level professionals. Personik helps to guide candidate from vacation response to the end of first three month in company. Using Personik via mobile messengers candidate can apply to vacation, get through online test, schedule interview, get job offer. Personik provides to a new employee essential information about workplace, answer questions and collect feedback.

    • Save up to 30 minutes per candidate application for recruitment manager at pre-screening stage
    • Answer more than 2 000 questions from employees
    • Increase engagement and retention for new employees
    • Gather feedback from candidates and employees to improve candidate journey map
    Wertex
    Wertex is a pharmaceutical company based in St. Petersburg, Russia. It employs over 1 500 workers, hiring over 300 professionals in R&D, manufacturing and sales every year.

    Efficient employee onboarding is one of the key priorities for the HR department. Thus a digital assistant Verta was launched in April 2020. Verta helps employees, managers and mentors to coordinate during the onboarding to improve the efficiency and reduce friction during the employee's first months in the company.

    So far Verta has helped to onboard 150 employees with overall NPS of 9.2 from the employees and 8.9 from the managers.
